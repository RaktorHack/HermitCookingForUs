Hermit is a weekly
meal prep service


We are a Mexican family with a tradition of service with more
than 30 years of experience, which seeks to provide the experience
of tasting the cuisine of the Mexican Caribbean and the Yucatan Peninsula.





Present in their memories since 1993, the year where the adventure of the weekly kitchen service began, carried out by our first and second generation.




This group of collaborators made up of a kitchen team, a service team and customer service are in charge of carrying out the kitchen service that you and your family were looking for during your vacations on the island of Cozumel.
